Our Mission

ACC Explorer was created to help students at Purdue better understand the wide range of technology-related majors available to them.

Many students struggle with choosing a major because programs can feel confusing or overwhelming. This platform simplifies that process by breaking down each major into clear categories such as skills, coursework, and career paths.

Our goal is to make academic planning easier, more visual, and more interactive so students can make confident decisions about their future.

Key Goals:
  • Help students explore tech majors in one place
  • Simplify complex program information
  • Support better academic decision-making
  • Provide an interactive learning experience
What We Offer

ACC Explorer provides students with an interactive way to explore technology programs at Purdue University Indianapolis.

Main Features:
  • Interactive Major Cards: Each program includes descriptions, skills, and career opportunities.
  • Career Matching Quiz: A guided quiz helps students discover which major best fits their interests and strengths.
  • Program Breakdowns: Detailed information about coursework, tools, and job pathways for each field.
  • User-Friendly Navigation: Simple layout designed to make exploring majors fast and intuitive.

This platform is designed to reduce confusion and help students confidently choose a direction in technology and computing fields.
